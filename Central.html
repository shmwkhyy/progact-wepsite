3-Winter Wonderland

If you are looking for an amusement park to gather the family, you will not find a better theme park than Winterland Riyadh, as the latter is one of the most famous and prominent amusement parks within the city of Riyadh in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, as there are many activities and wonderful entertainment shows that suit children and families to spend Fun and fun times during summer holidays and seasonal vacations while ensuring the most beautiful atmosphere full of joy and happiness

4-Riyadh season

Riyadh Season (Arabic: موسم الرياض) is a state-sponsored annual entertainment and sports festival, part of the larger Saudi Seasons initiative held since 2019 from October to March during winter in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia.

Al-Qassim

2-Prince Faisal bin Bandar Al Qassim Park

Prince Faisal Park is one of the largest tourist destinations in Qassim, due to its vast areas of green areas that it covers. It also includes special events on public occasions and holidays. Within the park there is a walking track, a children's play area, as well as kiosks that provide visitors with many products, fast food, and others

1-Buraidah Dates Festival:

The Dates Festival is held in Buraidah as an annual celebration by farmers, and it lasts for 75 days, one of the types of dates that you find at the Buraidah Festival is the Sukkari date, which is one of the most famous Qassim dates.
