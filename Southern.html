Al-Mandaq Governorate is located on the heights of the Hijaz Mountains overlooking the Tihama region. As for the climate, the monsoon winds blow in the summer and the opposite in the winter. In some seasons, dry eastern winds blow on it, and its rains are generally heavy, which allows the growth of vegetation cover, especially in the slopes and western mountains of the governorate.
